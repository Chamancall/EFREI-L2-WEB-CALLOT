"Welcome to the Velvet Room. This place exists between dream and reality, mind and matter." - Igor

Present in every Persona games, the Velvet Room is described as a room located between consciousness and subconsciousness.
Despite the change of its appearance in every iteration, it always stays with its blue color but in Persona 5, the Velvet Room takes the form of a prison according to the general theme of the rules and society's expactations.
It is always managed by Igor, a bizarre old man with a long nose, pointed ears and bulging, bloodshot eyes. Despite the appearances, Igor seems to be quite tall and he stands up. Even if he's present in every games, not much is known about him and his presence in the Velvet Room
and let the player make the acquaintance of Caroline and Justine, the assistants of Igor, which act like your correctional officers and regularly call you Inmate.
